to get the x coordinate for the centroid, given two functions $f(x), g(x)$ that create the shape:
Area $A=\ f(x)-g(x)dx$
$x=\frac{1}{A}*\int x(f(x)-g(x))dx$
$y=\frac{1}{2A}\int f^{2}(x) - g^{2}(x)dx$
