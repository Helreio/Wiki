# Kayari Uran

Say dalin nu nabasa

Tan magmaliw ya pitek

Too, aso, o baka

Ondakat ya manlakep.

Ang lupa pag nabasa

At putik-putikan na

Tao, aso, o baka

Yapak ay magkasama.

When the soil is wet with rain

And the path has turned to mud

You'll see footprints there again

Of man, dog, cow in one pod.
