# Linaew

# Original

The fog comes

on little cat feet.

It sits looking

over harbor and city

on silent haunches

and then moves on.

# Translation

Onsasabi so linaew

ya singa manaakar ya pusa.

Onyuyurong

ed marendeen iran sikla to,

ombabantag ed baley tan pampang

insan onla la.

(Patalos na anlong nen Carl Sandburg ya "Fog")
