# Olimaw

Olimaw ya andeket

Tawen lay mansirunget

Oras lay ipapaway

Dika'y dalin tan bantay

---

An olimaw o so dark

Dark as the evening sky

It's time for them to come out

Dika'y dalin and bantay

---

MODIFIED

Pasatsat ya ambanget

Tawen lay mansirunget

Oras lay panletaw

Dika’y dalin, olimaw

Pasatsat na mabaho

Hapon ay gumuguho

Oras na ng paglitaw

Dika’y dalin, olimaw
